# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the voria community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Values

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all community members with respect and kindness
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together to build something great
- **Be Honest**: Communicate openly and transparently
- **Be Professional**: Maintain professionalism in all interactions

## Expected Behavior

All members are expected to:
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of different opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Report inappropriate behavior to maintainers

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, race, religion, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Unwarranted and repeated unsolicited messages or "spam"
- Violent threats or language directed at another person or group
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Direct Communication**: If you're comfortable, speak directly with the person involved
2. **Report to Maintainers**: Email srizd449@gmail.com with details including:
   - Description of the behavior
   - Context and location (issue, PR, discussion, etc.)
   - Who was involved
   - When it occurred

3. **Confidentiality**: Reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct
- Investigating reports fairly and without bias
- Taking appropriate corrective action
- Maintaining confidentiality of reporters

Violations may result in:
- Warning from the maintainer
- Temporary or permanent ban from the community
- Removal of comments or content

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community spaces (GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests)
- Official voria events and spaces
- When representing voria in public

## Acknowledgment
